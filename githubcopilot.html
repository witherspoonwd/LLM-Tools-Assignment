Save the Puppies - Mission Statement

About Us

Save the Puppies is an organization dedicated to rescuing homeless puppies and finding them their forever homes.

Our Goals

Our goal is to provide a safe, loving environment for puppies in need and to help them find a family that will love and care for them for the rest of their lives.

How We Work

We work with local shelters and rescue groups to identify puppies in need. We then provide them with the necessary medical care, love and attention they need until we can find them a suitable home.
