Save the Puppies

Our Mission Statement

At Save the Puppies, our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for puppies who have no homes, while we work tirelessly to find their forever homes.

Our Goals

Our primary goals are to:

  • Rescue and provide shelter for puppies in need.
  • Provide necessary medical care, including spaying/neutering and vaccinations.
  • Socialize and train puppies to prepare them for adoption.
  • Find loving, forever homes for each puppy in our care.

How We Work

Save the Puppies operates by taking in puppies from various situations, such as shelters, owner surrenders, and stray intakes. We provide them with a safe and comfortable space where they can receive medical care, socialization, and training.

Our team works diligently to assess each puppy's personality and needs to ensure they are matched with the most suitable forever home. We also provide post-adoption support to ensure a smooth transition for both the puppy and the adopter.

By choosing Save the Puppies, you're not only providing a second chance for a puppy in need, but you're also supporting our mission to make the world a better place for all puppies.
