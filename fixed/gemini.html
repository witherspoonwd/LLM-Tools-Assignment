Our Mission Statement

Save the Puppies is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing homeless puppies and finding them loving forever homes. We believe every puppy deserves a chance at a happy and fulfilling life. Fueled by compassion and a commitment to animal welfare, we work tirelessly to provide these vulnerable pups with the care, medical attention, and love they need to thrive.

Our Goals

  • Rescue abandoned or surrendered puppies from shelters and high-risk situations.
  • Provide comprehensive medical care, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and treatment of any health concerns.
  • Socialize puppies with other dogs and people to ensure a smooth transition into a forever home.
  • Carefully screen potential adopters to find loving, responsible families who can meet the needs of each puppy.
  • Educate the public about responsible pet ownership and the importance of adoption.

How We Work

We rely on a dedicated network of volunteers and foster families to provide temporary loving homes for the puppies while we search for their perfect match. We host adoption events and utilize social media to showcase our adoptable pups and connect them with potential families.

If you're interested in adopting a puppy, fostering a pup in need, or volunteering your time, we encourage you to visit our website or contact us for more information.
